About Us

Construction and Development Inspection Services is a privately held company located in central Indiana. We have been performing commercial and residential construction monitoring and inspections since 1994.

We have a solid track record working on multi-million dollar construction projects, with exposure to virtually every type of commercial, industrial, and residential construction. Our dedicated, experienced, and professional team is committed to fast, reliable, customer-focused service.
